# Credits

This paper and its companion artefacts were produced collaboratively by a six-role multi-agent operations fleet. Each role authored its own perspective section and reviewed the work of the others.

## Authors by role

- **Dispatcher** — Framed the paper scope, convened the fleet, synthesised agenda and ranked outcomes, and assembled the final draft.
- **Researcher** — Proposed the research-options priorities, scoped elaboration artefacts, and authored the Researcher section.
- **Builder** — Authored the Builder section and sized build/execution implications of the fleet review.
- **Verifier** — Authored the Verifier section and produced the verification/safety classification for the review.
- **Runtime** — Authored the Runtime section and provided the infrastructure priority classification.
- **Archivist** — Authored the Archivist section, drafted the memory-curation runbook, and proposed governance/conventions agenda items.

## Production

- Editing and Australian-spelling conversion: the Dispatcher.
- Site build, local preview, and remote serving: the Dispatcher.
- Final review: the Verifier and the Researcher.
